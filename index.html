💡 How to use Google Drive links for images & videos: 1. Upload your file to Google Drive
2. Right-click → Share → "Anyone with the link" → Copy link
3. Your link: https://drive.google.com/file/d/FILE_ID/view?usp=sharing
4. The admin panel auto-converts this — just paste as-is and click Apply / Save
5. For images (icon, profile photo): the link is converted to a direct thumbnail URL
6. For videos: the link is converted to an embed /preview URL

💾 To make everything permanent: After setting links, copy the values into SITE_CONFIG in the <script> section of the HTML so they survive a page refresh.
